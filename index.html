Our Story

A Culinary Journey Around The World

From Passion to Plate

Country in a Box was born from a simple yet powerful idea: what if you could travel the world through food without leaving your home? Founded in the heart of the Philippines, we started with a passion for authentic Filipino comfort food and an insatiable curiosity about the diverse culinary traditions that make our world so rich and flavorful.

We noticed something special happening at Filipino family tables – the joy of sharing meals, the stories told over steaming plates, and the way food brings generations together. We wanted to preserve that warmth and connection while opening doors to new experiences.

Our Philosophy

"Every dish tells a story. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the aromatic kitchens of India, from the pasta houses of Italy to the taco stands of Mexico – we bring authentic flavors to your table with respect, passion, and care."
